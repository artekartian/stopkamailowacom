FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do my emails pass through external servers?+
No. This is the key difference. Your mail never leaves your Microsoft 365 tenant. The signature is added by an Exchange Online mail rule — inside the Microsoft infrastructure you already have and trust. We do not redirect mail through external servers, we do not process message content and we have no access to your emails. Some signature platforms require routing all company mail through their own servers — meaning every email with customer data, quotes and invoices first goes to a third party. With us, that risk simply does not exist.
Where exactly is the signature added?+
For new messages the signature is added automatically server-side (Exchange Online). For replies and forwards, the employee copies the template and sets the signature in their Outlook.
Why don't I see the signature in the "Sent" folder?+
For new emails the signature is added by the mail server after the message is sent, not in Outlook. The copy in the "Sent" folder shows the version without the signature because Outlook doesn't "know" it yet. The recipient, however, sees the full, professional signature.
Is the signature added to replies and forwarded messages?+
The automatic signature applies to new messages. For replies and forwarded messages (RE:, FW:) the employee can insert the signature manually — just copy it once from a received message and set it in Outlook Signatures. We provide a simple guide.
What Microsoft 365 data is populated?+
We support over 23 attributes, including: Full name, First name, Last name, Title, Department, Company name, Business phone, Mobile phone, Address, City, Postal code, Email address and many more. Just fill in the data in Entra ID.
Do I need a special Microsoft 365 license?+
No. Exchange Online mail rules are available in every Microsoft 365 plan that includes Exchange Online — from Business Basic to Business Premium and E3/E5. You do not need an additional license.
